Recipe for Tea 🍵

Ingredients 📃

  • Tea Powder
  • Sugar
  • Milk

Instructions 📃

  1. Take the amount of milk you wanna have and boil it
  2. Add in 2 tea-spoons of Tea powder of your choice
  3. Add in a tea spoon of sugar
  4. Boil it for 5-6 mins
  5. Your tea is ready!

Enjoy sippping your tea darlin 💅🏻
